Team Work

Even though most of the time I am a single player, I like to work on my own and get everything done by myself, I am also a team player.

I believe that, being part of a team, you learn to better understand the wishes and needs of others as well as your own.

Also it is a good opportunity to learn new ways to address a problem.

You know how they say, two heads are better than one.

Loving and protecting animals

One of the most beautiful and relaxing moments are those I spend with my pets.

I have always fought and will continue to fight for the protection of animals.

My bachelor's thesis consisted of a web application intended for the adoption of animals but also to protect them through different ways,by reporting an abuse or abandonment. I think there are various ways, many undiscovered or not yet implemented,through which technology can help to protect them.
